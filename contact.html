FAQ

How fast can an automation launch?

Starter workflows can launch in one to two weeks after assets, access, and workflow rules are confirmed.

Can you integrate with our current CRM?

Yes. We commonly connect HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Airtable, Notion, Google Sheets, Slack, and custom APIs.

Do we need to expose API keys in the website?

No. Secrets belong in your provider dashboard, serverless functions, or environment variables. They should never be committed to frontend code.

Yes. Human-in-the-loop approval can be included for sales, support, or sensitive workflows.
